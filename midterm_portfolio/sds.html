Seven Deadly Sins

cooperated with Luna Miao

December, 2013

This is the first ambitious paper art project. I and my partener inspired by the concept of seven deadly sins in the movie and try to express it in our own ways and finally we get this. It's a seven page popup book. In the first page, we use a man hang on the clock to indicate sloth. In the second page, there is a fat guy who even have a mouth oon his body, which expresses gluttony. In the third page, a man trapped deeply in the money means greedy. On the forth pgae, it tells you lust may be related with death. On the fifth page, an angle can even be transformed into evils if he has envy in his heart. ON the six page, it tells you people are pride is not a good thing. ON the final page, a fist deliver the feeling of warth.
